About Us

Supporting You with Dignity and Care

For nearly two decades, Lesedi Life Holdings has stood as a beacon of light and comfort for families across South Africa during their most delicate moments. Rooted in compassion, dignity, and excellence, our funeral services are tailored to support, guide, and honour the lives of those who have passed, while bringing comfort to those who remain.

Vision Statement

To be South Africa’s most trusted and compassionate funeral service provider, bringing light and hope to families during their time of loss through excellence, integrity, and care.

Mission Statement

At Lesedi Life Holdings, we are committed to delivering professional, respectful, and heartfelt funeral services that honour life and support grieving families.

We aim to:

  • Serve with dignity and empathy
  • Provide personalized, quality funeral packages
  • Be present before, beside, and behind our clients—every step of the way
  • Light homes and hearts through every service we offer

Core Values

  • Compassion: We treat every family with kindness and care
  • Excellence: We deliver services of the highest standard
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and respect in every interaction
  • Support: We walk with our clients from the first call to the final farewell
  • Dignity: We honour every life with the respect it deserves
